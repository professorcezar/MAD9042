# What the heck is a product manager anyway, and why should I care?

An introduction to product management - what it is, what they do, and why you should care about it.

# Speaker: Jon Harmer

Jon is a product manager at Google on the Google Cloud team, working with Google’s acquisitions and “other bets”. Previously at Google, Jon worked on the Gmail team, where his team built platform and extensibility features that enable rich integrations with Google Workspace, including AMP for Email and Google Workspace Add-ons. Prior to Google, Jon worked for 20+ years as an executive leading product management and marketing teams for companies including Nexidia, EyeWonder, and Verio. He serves as a mentor to startups at Atlanta Tech Village, Techstars, and Google For Startups and he co-founded LaaSer Critical Communications. Jon holds an MBA from The Goizueta Business School at Emory University, as well as a Bachelors of Science in Management from the Georgia Institute of Technology.
